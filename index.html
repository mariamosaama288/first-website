Ancient Egypt

Ancient Egypt was a civilization of ancient Northeast Africa. It was concentrated along the lower reaches of the Nile River, situated within the contemporary territory of modern-day Egypt.

History

Ancient Egypt lasted for over 3,000 years. It was one of the greatest civilizations in history.

Famous Monuments

The Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and Karnak Temple are some of the most iconic landmarks.

Culture

Ancient Egyptians were known for their art, religion, and scientific advancements.
